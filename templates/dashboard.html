🎯 Consensus Trend Analysis Methodology

📊 Trend Score Components (0-100):

  • MA Alignment (25%): Price position vs 50/150/200-day MAs + MA hierarchy + MA trend direction
  • Volume Strength (20%): Recent volume vs average + volume on up days vs down days
  • Momentum (20%): Multi-timeframe price performance (5, 10, 20, 50 days)
  • Relative Strength (20%): Performance vs SPY benchmark over multiple periods
  • Stage Analysis (15%): Weinstein-style market stage identification

📈 Timeframe Analysis:

  • Short-term (20d): Price above/below 20-day MA + MA direction
  • Medium-term (50d): Price above/below 50-day MA + MA direction
  • Long-term (200d): Price above/below 200-day MA + MA direction

🎯 Trend Ratings:

  • Strong Bullish (80-100): Low risk, all systems aligned
  • Bullish (65-79): Moderate risk, mostly positive signals
  • Neutral Bullish (50-64): Mixed signals, slight bullish bias
  • Neutral Bearish (35-49): Mixed signals, slight bearish bias
  • Bearish (20-34): High risk, mostly negative signals
  • Strong Bearish (0-19): Very high risk, all systems negative

🚨 Active Signals:

  • Breakouts: New 20-day highs on volume
  • MA Signals: Price above rising moving averages
  • Volume Alerts: Institutional interest detection

Methodology combines techniques from Mark Minervini (SEPA), Brian Shannon (multi-timeframe), William O'Neil (CAN SLIM), and Stan Weinstein (stage analysis).
